I decided to not put in music yet. I need to polish things up and make sure I’m not making anything annoying. I might wait until I have sound effects to put music in. And to make sound effects, I want to have a final version of what the game will play like. So I’m now trying to decide on how much I’ll prettify and animate the game. This is a hard decision for me because I don’t want to spend a long time doing this. So I’ll try to be very selective about what I polish. I guess the criteria should be:

  • How easily reusable is this improvement for other projects?
  • How fast can I make it?
  • Do I have a good reference to use when making the polish?
  • Does the polish look good on trailers?
  • Does the polish make the game easier to understand?
    • makes it easier for people who just started to understand the game
    • visualized that actions happened
    • how actions actually work
  • How often will new players see this polish?
  • how often will long-term players see this polish?
  • Is the polish making the game prettier but less enjoyable to play somehow? _____________________
