May 2nd Week - The Road to Steam Next Fest (II)

09 May 2022

I did release the new story this Friday, which I didn’t think would happen! So that is nice. Right now I’m adding equipment to support different types of builds (counter-attack, magic, extreme speed, extreme attack). This will add some build variation and complexity to the game. After that is done, I’ll try to introduce complexity a bit better by limiting what players can see at the start of the game. Right now very complex pieces of equipment can drop in the very first battle. While fun, that can be daunting. After that I think I’ll just polish the demo.

You can try the game here: https://pidroh.github.io/GenericRPGIdle/
