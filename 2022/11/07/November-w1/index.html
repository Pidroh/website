Next update:

  • Sounds and music, new animations and effects

The polish criteria is still this:

  • How easily reusable is this improvement for other projects?
  • How fast can I make it?
  • Do I have a good reference to use when making the polish?
  • Does the polish look good on trailers?
  • Does the polish make the game easier to understand?
    • makes it easier for people who just started to understand the game
    • visualized that actions happened
    • how actions actually work
  • How often will new players see this polish?
  • how often will long-term players see this polish?
  • Is the polish making the game prettier but less enjoyable to play somehow?

But I found a sound guy and want to get it done. So I don’t think I’ll be adding any additional effects other than what I have planned. Might enhance what is already there though. Maybe that’s more of sequel material though. _____________________
