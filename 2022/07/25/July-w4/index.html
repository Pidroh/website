GENERIC RPG WEEKLY REPORT

I decided to change directions. I decided on deprioritizing the story. So I won’t work on the story much until I can improve some of the things I’m not happy about in the game. Isabel’s story is a very small part of the GRI experience and You’s gameplay (I’m thinking of changing the name to Yuu) is pretty much the main meat of the game. I want to prioritize that first. I think that will result in a more engaging game and lay the fundation for improving both active gameplay and idle gameplay.

Right now I think that update 1 MIGHT have: A) Visual effects B) New exploration system C) more Unknown tab content D) Improved selling (maybe) E) New items and skills (maybe)

While update 2 MIGHT have: A) Visual effects and sound effects lol possibly music B) Chapter 2 maybe C) Offline progress and XP cap removal (maybe maybe)
